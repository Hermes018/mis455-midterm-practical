Contact Us

We are always thrilled to hear from fellow musicians! Whether you have a question about a specific guitar, need advice on building your pedalboard, or want to inquire about our repair services, our friendly and experienced staff are here to help. At Guitars n Gears, your musical journey is our top priority.

Visit our flagship store located right in the heart of the city. You can find us at: 123 Elephant Road, Dhaka, Bangladesh. We are open from 10:00 AM to 8:00 PM, Saturday through Thursday. Feel free to drop by to try out any gear before making a purchase. We have dedicated soundproof rooms for testing amplifiers and pedals.

If you cannot make it to the store, you can also reach us via phone at +880 1234 567890 or email us at support@guitarsngears.com.bd. Follow us on our social media channels to stay updated on new arrivals, community workshops, and exclusive discounts. We look forward to connecting with you!
